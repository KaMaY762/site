💡 Tips for Parents

⏰
Keep it short
Start with 5–10 minute sessions. Short, positive experiences build confidence over time.
🎉
Celebrate effort
Focus on participation, not perfection. Every small try deserves a cheer!
🔄
Use routines
Doing activities at the same time each day helps children feel safe and ready to engage.
🧸
Follow their lead
Let your child guide the pace. If they're not ready, try again tomorrow — no pressure.
